Introduction

元话 RWA（MetaKina）由西安西西弗斯网络科技有限公司开发，致力于通过区块链和人工智能技术，实现现实世界资产的代币化与高效交易。平台采用SaaS 多租户架构，满足 90% 的中小微组织完成 RWA 数字资产的发行和融资需求。

MetaKina RWA, developed by Xi'an Sisyphus Network Technology Co., Ltd., is committed to realizing the tokenization and efficient trading of real-world assets through blockchain and AI technologies. The platform employs a SaaS multi-tenant architecture to meet the issuance and financing needs of 90% of small and medium-sized organizations for RWA digital assets.

Product Description

MetaKina RWA integrates the security and transparency of blockchain with the intelligent analysis capabilities of AI, employing a SaaS multi-tenant architecture to provide users with digital asset management, trading, and intelligent analysis. The platform enables small and medium-sized organizations to issue and finance RWA digital assets with low cost and high efficiency.
